Purpose

Our main purpose is to promote the cultural learning of our country. We must provoke the interaction between the exhibition and the visitor. see, hear, touch, feel. Experimenting for oneself is the best learning experience we can do.

Our second purpose is to be able to remember our ancestors through the memories that they have left us in our lands, culture and origin.

Information

Audience

The museum is open to all families, everyone who wants to know our origins through history can visit the museum, totally free.
